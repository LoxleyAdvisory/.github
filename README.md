## What We Do

**Technical Due Diligence**
Pre-acquisition and post-LOI assessments that surface integration risk, architectural debt, and talent concentration before they become your problem. Structured for speed without sacrificing depth.

**Fractional CTO / CIO Leadership**
Senior technology leadership on a fractional basis. Embedded, accountable, and exit-ready. Not advisory from a distance. Actual ownership of the technology function.

**Technical Health Assessments**
Structured evaluation of architecture, team, data posture, and change readiness. Produces a clear Red/Yellow/Green picture that leadership can act on.

**AI Strategy and Readiness**
Beyond the hype. We assess whether your organization is actually ready to extract value from AI investment: governance, architecture, and the human factors most firms ignore.
